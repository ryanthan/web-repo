Super Mario

Who is he?

Mario (a.k.a. Super Mario, Jumpman, Mr. Video) is a fictional character created by Shigeru Miyamoto. He is the titular character of the Mario franchise of video games. He resides in the Mushroom Kingdom and is often seen going on adventures to rescue his love interest Princess Peach from the nefarious Koopa king, Bowser. He has appeared in over 200 video games to date including both 2D/3D platformers, sports games, fighting games, and much more. His very first appearance was in Donkey Kong (1981) and at the time of writing, his latest appearance was in Mario Golf: Super Rush (2021).
