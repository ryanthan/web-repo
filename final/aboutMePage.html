Hello there! I am currently a fifth-year Computer Science student with a minor in Creative Technology & Design. After graduation, I will be a software engineer at Lingoport, a local software company in Boulder, Colorado.

I love working on the prototyping, design (front-end, UI/UX), and programming of software projects including websites, mobile applications, and more. I have learned and am proficient in various coding languages such as Python, C/C++, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Swift, and Kotlin. I also have experience with design programs such as Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator, Clip Studio Paint, Procreate, and several others. I am always eager to learn and am currently teaching myself Java, C#, and Unity.

My career interests lie primarily in creative yet technical areas of Computer Science. I enjoy web development and mobile app design as they allow me to take some creative liberties while also allowing me to practice my technical coding abilities. I am especially passionate about video games. From the design choices to the inner workings of the source code, I strive to one day create video games that can be enjoyed by anyone.
